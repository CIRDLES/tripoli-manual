The arithmetic mean:
$$ \bar{x} = \sum_{i=1}^n x_i $$

The variance
$$ \sigma^2 = \sum_{i=1}^n \big( x_i - \bar{x}\big)^2 $$

The standard deviation
$$ \sigma = \sqrt{\sigma^2} $$ 

The standard error
$$ \sigma_{\bar{x}} = \dfrac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}} $$
